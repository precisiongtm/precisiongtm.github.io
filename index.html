How this shows up in practice

US Market Entry

Problem
Insurance AI platform with success in Asia and Europe with multiple entry motions. Unclear which approach will scale in the US.
What changed
  • Analyzed pipeline for patterns across various use cases (claims, underwriting, etc.)
  • Compared US and comparable EU deals for buyer patterns
  • Identified best entry point for US growth
Outcome
Clear recommendation on which approach to prioritize and where to efficiently focus sales and marketing resources.

Scaling Growth Without Increasing Costs

Problem
Construction tech AI company needed to triple ARR in one year without increasing sales and marketing spend. Lacked systematic approach to deploy resources efficiently.
What changed
  • Analyzed 50 recent deals to refine ICP in contractor segment
  • Built account scoring system to prioritize 300 top targets from 3,000 accounts
  • Created repeatable outbound motion with target personas, entry messages, and weekly cadence
  • Established tracking for early signs of improving pipeline quality
Outcome
Account scoring model and refined ICP that aligned paid media spend with sales targeting. Clear system for efficient resource deployment.

Clarifying Category Narrative to Reduce Sales Cycle

Problem
Employee productivity AI company with unclear category and monitoring perception issues. Sales cycles were long, reps struggled with tribal knowledge, and "employee monitoring" narrative triggered cultural objections.
What changed
  • Analyzed website, case studies, and sales conversations to identify narrative friction
  • Reframed category from "monitoring" to "operational intelligence for service delivery"
  • Built sales playbook with objection handling for privacy and culture concerns
  • Created SDR messaging framework and discovery questions for US buyers
Outcome
Clear US category narrative, repeatable sales story, and tools to address monitoring objections early. Reduced explanation burden and improved conversion rates.
